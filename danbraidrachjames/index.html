Westown Bend
Westown Bend arrived on Cleveland's music scene in the Spring of 2019, gathering a following and packing local venues such as The Foundry, Mahall's, Coda and Stella's Music Club.

Proudly hailing from one of the best kept secrets in the US, Westown Bend delivers hard-hitting heartfelt, genuine realness in every song they play. WB's sound, which lies on the outs of Folk, Indie and Alternative will find you unable to put them in one specific box.

If the harmonies don't get you, the beat will.
